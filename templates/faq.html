Frequently Asked Questions

What type of images does the model support?

The system supports standard bone X-ray images (JPEG or PNG format). Images may include anatomical regions such as the elbow, shoulder, wrist, or hand.

How does the fracture detection work?

The uploaded X-ray image is processed using a deep learning model based on convolutional neural networks (CNNs). The model analyzes visual patterns such as bone alignment and texture changes to classify the image as Fractured or Normal.

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. This application is intended for educational and research purposes only. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or clinical decision-making. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
